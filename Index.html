HISTORY OF OUR SCHOOL

With the vision of providing quality education to the local community. What began as a small educational institution with a handful of students has grown into one of the most respected schools in the region. Over the years, the school has continuously adapted to meet the evolving needs of its students, while upholding its core values of academic excellence, integrity, and community service.
